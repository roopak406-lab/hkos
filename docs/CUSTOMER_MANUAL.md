# HKOS — Customer Guide

Ordering from your home kitchen takes under a minute. No app to download, no
account to create.

---

## 1. Open the menu
Tap the kitchen’s link (shared on WhatsApp), e.g.
`https://…/k/aromatic-tadka`. You’ll see **tomorrow’s** freshly-planned menu.

## 2. Add what you like
- Browse **Breakfast, Lunch, Evening, Pickles**.
- Some dishes have choices (e.g. Paneer / Aloo / Gobi) — tap your variant.
- Tap **Add**, then use **–/+** to set the quantity.
- If a dish says **“3 left”**, that’s the limited fresh batch for the day.

## 3. Checkout
Tap the **Checkout** bar at the bottom. Fill in:
- **Name**, **Mobile number**
- **Flat number** and **Tower/Block**
- **Delivery slot** (Breakfast / Lunch / Evening)
- *(Optional)* Special instructions (“less spicy”, “no onion”)

## 4. Pay via UPI
After you tap **Place order**, you’ll see your **order number** and the
kitchen’s **UPI ID**. Pay the amount shown and keep the reference handy for
delivery. You can also message the kitchen on WhatsApp from the confirmation
screen.

---

## Good to know
- **Order by 6:00 PM** for next-day delivery.
- Everything is **freshly cooked in limited batches** — order early for the best
  choice.
- Delivery is currently **within Fortuna Krrish Apartment**.
- Your details are remembered on your phone for faster reordering; they’re only
  shared with the kitchen you order from.

Enjoy your homemade meal! 🍽️
