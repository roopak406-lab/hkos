# HKOS — Owner / Admin Manual

Welcome! HKOS is built so you spend **under 5 minutes a day** managing your
kitchen. This guide walks through everything, in plain language.

---

## Signing in
Go to **/login**, enter your email and password. You’ll land on the
**Dashboard**. On your phone, the main sections sit in the bottom bar.

---

## Your daily routine (the 5-minute flow)

1. **Open Menu** (bottom bar → *Menu*).
2. **Tick the dishes** you’ll cook tomorrow. Pickles that are “Always” are
   pre-ticked.
3. *(Optional)* Type a **price** for the day, or a **Qty** to cap the batch
   (great for “no waste” — the item auto-marks sold-out when it runs out).
4. Tap **Publish menu**.
5. Tap **Copy message** and paste it into your WhatsApp group. Done. ✅

Your storefront link is `yoursite.com/k/aromatic-tadka` — pin it in the group.

---

## Dashboard
Your morning glance:
- **Today’s revenue, orders, pending, preparing**
- **Today’s profit** (revenue − expenses) and **average order value**
- **Best-seller** and **repeat customers**
- **Tomorrow’s menu** status with a one-tap link to publish/edit

---

## Orders
A live board of **today + tomorrow’s** orders.
- Each card shows the customer, flat/tower, items, total, and payment status.
- Tap the **big button** to move an order forward:
  *New → Accepted → Preparing → Ready → Delivered*.
- **Mark paid** once the UPI payment lands.
- **Cancel** removes it from revenue and the prep list.
- Use **search** (name, phone, order number, flat) and the **status filters**.
- Tap a phone number to **call** the customer directly.

---

## Kitchen
Open this when you start cooking.
- **Preparation list**: total quantity of each dish to cook (all orders summed).
- **Packing list**: one line per order, with the flat/tower to deliver to.
- Toggle **Today / Tomorrow**.

---

## Finance
- **Revenue / Expenses / Profit** for the month, with a daily chart.
- **Log an expense**: pick a category (Groceries, Vegetables, Oil, Gas,
  Packaging, Misc), enter the amount, date, and an optional note. Tap **Add**.
- See totals **by category** and your **recent expenses**.

---

## Customers
Your little black book, built automatically from orders.
- Sorted by who orders most. **Repeat** customers are badged.
- Each card shows order count, lifetime spend, last order, and their
  **favourite dish**.
- Search by name, phone, or flat.

---

## Menu — product library
- **New product**: name, price, category, description. It joins the library and
  can be added to any day’s menu.
- Prices you type on the publish screen are **one-day overrides**; the product’s
  default price is untouched.

---

## Tips
- **Order cutoff** is 6:00 PM today for tomorrow’s delivery — publish before then.
- Set **Qty caps** to avoid over-cooking; the storefront shows “N left” and
  blocks over-ordering.
- The storefront and WhatsApp message always reflect your **latest publish**.

Questions or a new dish idea? Everything is editable — you can’t break it. 🍲
